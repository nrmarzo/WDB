Darrow Darrow is the main protagonist and sole narrator of the first Red Rising Trilogy. His experiences throughout the trilogy transform him from a lowly Red Helldiver into the legendary leader of The Rising, using his education as a Gold to earn numerous supporters from the various classes of society.
Sevro au Barca Sevro is a Gold and one of the main characters of the Red Rising trilogy and the second trilogy. Throughout the course of the books, Sevro is one of Darrow's most loyal followers, and is leader of Darrow's Howlers.
Ragnar Volarus Ragnar Volarus is a Stained, the class of the strongest and deadliest of the Obsidians. He is the son of Alia Snowsparrow, the Queen of the Valkyrie and the leader of the Obsidians, and brother of Sefi the Quiet. He had been a slave to the Golds for more than 25 years, during which his sister Sefi had vowed to remain silent, thus earning her nickname. He was freed by Darrow in Golden Son. Darrow explained to Ragnar that he was a Red. He explained how he was carved into a Gold; this caused Ragnar to declare loyalty to Darrow and his cause.
